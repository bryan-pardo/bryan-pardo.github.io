I head the Interactive Audio Lab. We make machines that hear. Ongoing research in the lab is applied to audio scene labeling, audio source separation, inclusive interfaces, new audio production tools and machine audition models that learn without supervision.

When I'm not being an academic, I tend to be making music with groups like Son Monarcas and The East Loop.
