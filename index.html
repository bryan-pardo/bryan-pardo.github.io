I head the Interactive Audio Lab. We develop new methods in Machine Learning, Signal Processing and Human Computer Interaction to make new tools for finding, separating, understanding and manipulating sound.

I co-direct the Northwestern University Center for Human Computer Interaction + Design.

I am the faculty advisor for Latin@CS and chair the CS Diversity Committee.

When I'm not being an academic, I make music with the Latin Folk Fusion ensemble Son Monarcas.
